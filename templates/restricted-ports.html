Restricted Ports

Notes:

  • Limit Regex is a Perl regular expression used to limit the port's version.
  • Limit Even/Odd tells portscout to limit the (n+1)th number to even or odd numbers only.
  • Ports with a version number in their name are automatically dealt with.

Please let me know if any of these are incorrect or change.
